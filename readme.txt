####### Required Installs ########
OpenGL 4.1 or newer.
The GLFW3 library.

####### Controls #########
Camera control:
Use the arrow keys to turn around the focal point of the camera.
Use W and S to shift the camera up or down.
Interactivity:
Hold V to sway gravity slightly left to right.
Press R to position the moving solid ball against the ceiling of the aquarium.
Hold F to gradually turn on faucets.
Hold G to gradually turn off faucets.
